# Theory

The **DJI Phantom 4 Pro** is an advanced photography drone released in November 2016.  
It is equipped with several key components that ensure high-quality performance and reliability:

## Components and Their Functions

1. **Camera System**
   - Equipped with a **1-inch (25 mm) 20 MP CMOS sensor (FC6310)**.  
   - Captures high-resolution images and 4K video recording.  
   - Ensures professional-grade photography and videography.

2. **Gimbal System**
   - A **three-axis stabilized gimbal camera**.  
   - Keeps the camera stable even when the drone experiences vibrations or sudden movements.  
   - Provides smooth, shake-free footage.

3. **Obstacle Avoidance Sensors**
   - Includes **five-directional obstacle avoidance sensors**.  
   - Helps the drone automatically detect and avoid obstacles during flight.  
   - Increases flight safety and reduces the chances of crashing.

4. **Remote Controllers**
   - Two types are available:  
     - **Phantom 4 Pro+** – comes with a built-in display screen.  
     - **Phantom 4 Pro** – requires connection to a mobile device for live viewing.  
   - Enables the pilot to control drone movements, camera, and flight functions.

5. **Propulsion System**
   - Uses **propellers powered by brushless motors**.  
   - Brushless motors are efficient, durable, and provide strong lift and stability.  
   - Essential for smooth flight operations.

6. **Flight Controller System**
   - Acts as the "brain" of the drone.  
   - Processes input from the remote controller, GPS, sensors, and motors.  
   - Ensures stable flight and responsive control.

7. **GPS-Based Navigation System**
   - Provides accurate positioning and stability.  
   - Enables intelligent flight modes like waypoint navigation, return-to-home (RTH), and orbit mode.  
   - Crucial for both beginners and professional pilots.

## Working Principle

- The drone works by combining the **flight controller, GPS, sensors, motors, and gimbal system**.  
- The **remote controller (transmitter)** sends signals to the drone for altitude, direction, and camera control.  
- The **flight controller** interprets these signals and adjusts motor speeds accordingly.  
- **GPS and sensors** ensure stable flight, while the gimbal maintains smooth footage.  
- The live video feed can be viewed on the built-in screen or a connected mobile device.

## Applications

Camera drones like the **Phantom 4 Pro** are widely used in:  
- **Aerial Photography and Filmmaking** – capturing cinematic shots.  
- **Surveying and Mapping** – creating 2D/3D maps.  
- **Agriculture** – crop monitoring and analysis.  
- **Inspection Tasks** – infrastructure, power lines, and buildings.  
- **Search and Rescue Operations** – locating missing persons or monitoring disaster areas.

This detailed explanation of the components and working makes the functioning of the Phantom 4 Pro clear to users and highlights its importance in real-world applications.
