## Theory
The **DJI Phantom 4 Pro** is an advanced photography drone released in
November 2016. It is equipped with:\
- A **1-inch (25 mm) 20 MP CMOS sensor (FC6310)** for high-quality
photography and video recording.\
- A **three-axis stabilized gimbal camera** that ensures smooth and
stable footage.\
- **Five-directional obstacle avoidance sensors** to enhance flight
safety.\
- Two types of remote controllers -- one with a dedicated screen
(Phantom 4 Pro+) and one without.

The drone works by using its **propellers powered by brushless motors**,
a **flight controller system**, and a **GPS-based navigation system**
for stable flying. The transmitter (remote controller) communicates with
the drone to control altitude, direction, and camera functions. The live
video feed can be viewed on the transmitter screen or connected mobile
device.

Camera drones like Phantom 4 Pro are widely used in **aerial
photography, filmmaking, surveying, mapping, agriculture, and inspection
tasks**.
