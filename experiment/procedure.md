## Procedure
**Step 1: Setup**\
1. Place the drone, propellers, and controller on a flat table.\
2. Attach all four propellers to the drone.\
3. Insert the battery into the drone securely.

**Step 2: Controller & Activation**\
1. Power on the remote controller.\
2. Turn on the drone.\
3. On the transmitter screen, sign in to your **DJI account**.\
4. Activate the drone system.\
5. Select the camera option and use the transmitter for zoom and video
view.

**Step 3: Flying the Drone**\
1. Carry the drone outside or to an open area where filming is
required.\
2. Place it on a flat surface for take-off.\
3. Use the **throttle stick** to lift the drone into the air.\
4. View the live feed/video footage on the transmitter.\
5. Control the drone movement by gently moving the throttle clockwise,
upward, or downward.\
6. Adjust the camera for desired filming angles.\
7. Land the drone safely by lowering the throttle stick.
